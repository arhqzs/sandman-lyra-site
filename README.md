# Sandman Lyra — The Living Universe of Sander Lylloff

Not a normal homepage. A living archive and digital home — part library, part
personal myth, part sports mind, part business platform. A place where a whole
life is slowly uploaded into one universe, and where people land and understand
Sandman in 60 seconds before the deeper rooms unfold behind it.

Tone: honest, warm, intelligent, cinematic, slightly mythological — *a man trying
to live truthfully, create beauty, build things, and do good.*

There is **nothing to install** and **no domain needed** to see it. It is plain
HTML, CSS and a little JavaScript — fast, secure, and easy to back up.

---

## 1. See it right now

Open the `Sandman Lyra` folder and **double-click `index.html`**. It opens in your
browser. Click the top menu to walk through every world. (Works on your phone too.)

## 3. Adding material (only you can)

The public side is **read-only** — visitors can read and reach out, but no one can
post their own material. Your easy "add" tool is private:

- Open **`studio.html`** (your hidden desk — not linked anywhere, ignored by Google).
  Pick the world, write, press send. It opens an email so the material reaches me.
- Or just send me text, photos, videos or voice notes any time.

I then place each piece in the right world, dated and backed up. Most pages show
*"coming soon"* placeholders — they're replaced as you send the real thing.

## 4. Backups & protection

- **Copy the folder** (one copy = a full backup). Keep one on your computer + one on a drive.
- **Cloud copy** (Dropbox / iCloud / Google Drive / OneDrive).
- **Private GitHub repo** (recommended next) — dated history of every change + off-site backup.
